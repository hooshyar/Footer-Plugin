First, what's this?

It's a third party jQuery plugin.

...hmm... and what it does?

This plugin ensures that the footer of your page always keeps itself at the bottom of the page.
If the body height is less than the page height, the footer goes to the bottom of the page.
Otherwise it remains attached at end of the content.

...wow... but if my page changes his size dynamically?

No matter, even whether the footer changes its size, the correct layout will be achieved.
